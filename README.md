I am an assistant professor at the University of Toronto, jointly appointed in the [Faculty of Information](https://ischool.utoronto.ca/profile/rohan-alexander/) and the [Department of Statistical Sciences](https://www.statistics.utoronto.ca/people/directories/all-faculty/rohan-alexander). I am also the assistant director of [CANSSI Ontario](https://canssiontario.utoronto.ca), a senior fellow at [Massey College](https://www.masseycollege.ca), a faculty affiliate at the [Schwartz Reisman Institute for Technology and Society](https://sr-institute.utoronto.ca/), and a co-lead of the [DSI](https://datasciences.utoronto.ca) Thematic Program in Reproducibility.

📚 My book on foundational data science, *Telling Stories With Data*, was published by Chapman and Hall/CRC in 2023. A free online copy is available [here](https://www.tellingstorieswithdata.com/), and a hard copy is available for purchase [here](https://www.routledge.com/Telling-Stories-with-Data-With-Applications-in-R/Alexander/p/book/9781032134772).

🧑‍💻 My research investigates how we can develop workflows that improve the trustworthiness of data science. I am particularly interested in the role of automated testing in data science.
